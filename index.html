Welcome to my Game

How to play:

  1. Click "Play": Start a new game session by clicking the "Play" button below.
  2. Type Your Guess: In the input box, type your guess for the word you think is correct.
  3. Submit Your Guess: Click the "Submit" button to check your answer.
  4. Check Your Guess: If a letter disappears from the input box, it means that letter is not in the word. If a letter appears in the dotted line, it means that letter is correct and in the right position.
  5. Keep Guessing: Continue typing and submitting guesses until you've correctly guessed the entire word.
  6. Use Hint (Optional): If you're stuck, click on the hint box for a clue to help you guess the word.
  7. Finish the Game: Once you've guessed the word correctly, the game will end. You can then choose to start a new game.
  8. ENJOY!
